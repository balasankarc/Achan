
mEdhaasooktham

mEdhaa dEvii jooshamaaNaa na aagaa
dvizaachii bhadraa sumanasyamaanaa
thvayaa jooshTaa nudamaanaa durukthaan
brhadvadEma vidathhE suviiraa:

thvayaa jushTa Rshir bhavathi dEvi
thvayaa brahmaagatha zriiruthathyayaa
thvaya jushTazchithram vindathE vasusaanO
juushasva draviNE na mEdhE
mEdhaamma indrO dadaathu mEdhaam dEvii sarasvathii
mEdhaammE azvinaavubhaavaadhaththaam pushkarasrajaa
apsaraasu yaa mEdhaa gandharvEshu cha yadyaza:
deiviim mEdhaa manushyajaa saamaam mEdhaa surabhirjushathaam
aamaam mEdhaa surabhir vizvaroopaa
hiraNyavarNaa jagathiijagamyaa
oorjasvathii payasaa pinvamaanaa
saa maam mEdhaa suprathiikaajushathaam
mayimEdhaam mayiprajaam mayi agni:thEjOdadhaathu
mayimEdhaam mayiprajaam mayi indra: indriyam dadhaathu
mayimEdhaam mayiprajaam mayi soorya:bhraajOdadhaathu
